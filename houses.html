Event Rules

To qualify as a social distance friendly Trick or Treating home you must agree to:
1. Wearing masks at all time
2. Maintaining a distance of minimum 2m between individuals
3. Regularly disinfecting frequently touched surfaces such as doorbells
4. Not incorporate individuals who have or may have been exposed to COVID-19
5. Have a fun spoOOoooky time!

You can place a table at your door with treats spread out on top!
View costumes at a distance! Please avoid allowing individuals into your home!
